# Workspaces & Projects

Workspaces and projects let you organize conversations, documents, and team
members into logical groups. A workspace is the top-level container that holds
all your resources, while projects provide finer-grained organization within a
workspace. This section explains how to create and manage both.

Effective organization keeps your team focused and ensures the right documents
and conversations are accessible to the right people. Whether you are working
alone or collaborating across departments, workspaces and projects help maintain
clear boundaries and context.

- **[Workspaces](workspaces.md)** -- Create and manage workspaces for your
  team
- **[Projects](projects.md)** -- Organize conversations and share resources
  within a workspace
