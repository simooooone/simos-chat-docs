# Documents & RAG

AirGap AI Chatbot uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to ground AI
responses in your own documents. Upload files to a workspace, let the system
index them, and then ask questions that are answered based on the content of
those documents. This section explains how to manage your document library and
query it effectively.

The RAG pipeline handles indexing, chunking, and embedding automatically. You
only need to upload your files and start asking questions. The Uploading guide
covers file formats, size limits, and the upload workflow, while the Querying
guide explains how to construct effective questions and interpret RAG-powered
responses.

- **[Uploading](uploading.md)** -- Upload and manage documents in your
  workspace
- **[Querying](querying.md)** -- Ask questions against your documents with
  retrieval-augmented generation
